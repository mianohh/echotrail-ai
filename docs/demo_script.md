## Key Demo Tips:
- **Smooth Recording**: 60fps screen capture with clean browser window
- **Real Interactions**: Show actual clicking, typing, and loading states
- **Highlight Innovation**: Emphasize the temporal-semantic clustering uniqueness
- **Show Polish**: Demonstrate animations, responsive design, and attention to detail
- **Clear Value**: Focus on emotional intelligence and self-understanding benefits
- **Strong CTA**: End with clear next steps for judges and users

## Technical Setup:
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 minimum
- **Browser**: Chrome with clean profile, no extensions visible
- **Audio**: Clear voiceover with background music at 20% volume
- **Timing**: Keep each scene tight, use smooth transitions
- **Branding**: Consistent EchoTrail AI branding throughout
